Mount Timpanogos Caves

Mount Timpanogos Caves is a national monument found in American Fork Canyon. There are many things they provide for entertainment. there's a visitors center where you can take a mini info tour, learn more about the park, talk with a ranger, or buy souvenirs. When you're ready to go you can start hiking along a paved path way 1.5 miles long. Along the trail kids can use a provided activity book and learn more about the nature surrounding them as you hike. When you will arrive you'll get the opportunity to get an actual tour through the caves and xperience what its like inside three different caves, and even experience total darkness. This is a safe fun activity for the whole family to participate in as long as no one is in any condition not to complete the tour.
